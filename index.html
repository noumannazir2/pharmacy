Jashh Pharma

Profile

Jassh Pharma is a specialized in Haemodialysis Concentrates of all types and formulation for all brands of Dialysis Machines like Gambro, Toray, Frezenuis, Baxter etc. It produces various types of Bicarbonate Haemodialysis Concentrates both in Powder & Liquid form. These products are being provided at a very nominal cost to dialysis centres.

In Pakistan there are approximately 30,000 new patients of end stage renal diseases and about 90% of the die within the first year due to poverty, ignorance and dialysis. The cost of the imported dialysis products is far from the reach of the patients. Therefore, it is the requirement of the day to provide low cost & high quality haemodialysis facilities in Pakistan.

History

In the past most of the patients were using Acetate Haemodialysis Solution because of high cost of Bicartbonate Haemodialysis Solutions available in the market and only 4-5% patients were getting Bicarbonate Haemodialysis. With the introduction of Bicarbonate Haemodialysis at very much lower cost now the patients are shifting from Acetate Dialysis to Bicarb Dialysis. Jassh Pharma has played an active role to educate the patients about the advantages of the Bicarbonate Haemodialysis over Acetate Haemodialysis. They are serving the ailing population by providing the high quality products at very much lower cost.

ISO Certificate

JASSH Pharma is the combined result of years of experience in research, development and global sales of innovative Dialysis and disinfection/ hygiene products for the healthcare/household and factories sector. JASSH Pharma, with its production site in Lahore, Pakistan, is the combined result of years of experience in research, development, production and global sales of innovative Dialysis and disinfection/ hygiene products for the healthcare/household and factories sector. The product portfolio includes modern dialysis solutions, modern cleaning and disinfection products and water treatment plants. JASSH PHARMA is ISO 13485, 14001, 9001 certified. The products come from modern and efficient production. JASSH PHARMA is a Pakistani company, with a connection to its production location.

Since the 2006 JASSH Pharma has been among the first Pakistani manufacturer of the medical devices and medicated cosmetics that reached the certification within the Pakistani medical devices regulation in accordance with DRAP’s medical devices rules 2012. Furthermore JASSH is already certified in accordance with ISO 13485:2016, 9001:2015 and 14001:2015.
